About Shegaw Engineering

Home / About Us

Engineering Excellence Since 2010

Founded on the principles of technical expertise, integrity, and client satisfaction, Shegaw Engineering has grown from a small maintenance contractor to a leading provider of comprehensive electromechanical services.

Our team of certified engineers and technicians brings together decades of combined experience in industrial automation, power systems, HVAC, and mechanical installations. We serve diverse sectors including manufacturing, healthcare, education, commercial real estate, and infrastructure.

What sets us apart is our commitment to staying ahead of industry trends. We continuously invest in training, technology, and tools to deliver solutions that are not just compliant with current standards, but anticipate future needs.
