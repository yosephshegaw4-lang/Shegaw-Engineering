System Installation

Complete design and installation of electromechanical systems including motors, drives, control panels, HVAC systems, pumps, and automated machinery. We ensure seamless integration with your existing infrastructure and strict adherence to industry standards and local codes.

  • Industrial motor installation
  • Control panel fabrication
  • HVAC system setup
  • Pump and compressor installation
🔧

Preventive Maintenance

Proactive maintenance programs designed to maximize equipment lifespan, minimize unexpected downtime, and optimize performance. Our scheduled inspections include lubrication, calibration, thermal imaging, vibration analysis, and component testing.

  • Scheduled inspections
  • Predictive maintenance
  • Performance optimization
  • Condition monitoring
📊

Diagnostics & Repair

Rapid troubleshooting and repair services using advanced diagnostic tools including multimeters, oscilloscopes, thermal cameras, and vibration analyzers. We identify root causes and implement permanent solutions to prevent recurrence.

  • 24/7 emergency response
  • Root cause analysis
  • Component repair/replacement
  • System restoration
🤖

Automation & Controls

Modernize your operations with cutting-edge automation solutions. We specialize in PLC programming, SCADA system integration, HMI design, robotics integration, and IoT sensor deployment for real-time monitoring and control.

  • PLC programming & troubleshooting
  • SCADA integration
  • HMI design
  • Industrial IoT solutions
📐

Design & Consulting

Expert consulting services for new construction and retrofit projects. We provide system design, load calculations, energy audits, code compliance reviews, and project management to ensure your project meets specifications and budget.

  • System design & engineering
  • Energy efficiency audits
  • Code compliance consulting
  • Project management
🛡️

Safety & Compliance

Comprehensive safety programs to protect your workforce and assets. We conduct electrical safety audits, arc flash studies, implement lockout/tagout procedures, and provide OSHA-compliant training for your maintenance teams.

  • Electrical safety audits
  • Arc flash analysis
  • LOTO procedures
  • Safety training programs
